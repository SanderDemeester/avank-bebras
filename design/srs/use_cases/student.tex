\begin{section}{Pupil}
	When the student logins in he will be redirected to his dashboard where he will find information about his current status in the system. He will be able to see
	\begin{itemize}
	\item His current class (the class he is associated with).
	\item The previous competitions he took part in.
	\item List of competitions where he/she can participate in (nl; Unrestricted contest, Restricted contest, Official contest)
	\item A view to his personal statistics (this includes his own level)
	\item His own preferred language
	\item View his own class
	\end{itemize}
